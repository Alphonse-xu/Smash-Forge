Jam's opinion: Suggestions and builds (NOT DISCUSSION)

Builds format: (Builds get pinned)
"Build [Date] Changes: [Changes/Things to test]"

Suggestion format: (Not strict but try to have the needed info :P)
"[Suggestion name]
[Description*]"

*If you are reporting an issue/suggesting a change please provide what currently happens and what you want/expect to happen.

Please no repeats of suggestions and please do not use the channel to discuss suggestions. Feel free to edit your suggestions after submission.

Examples:

Builds 1/10/2017 Changes: Add MTA GUI editing, requires further testing.

Suggestion: Add a path.bin editor
Just a simple list of frames and text boxes below to edit them.
Maybe some sort of curve editor as well?
